BCA-304:Numerical Methodology

Unit-1:Solution of a nonlinear algebraic anf transcendential equations: Bisection method, False position, Newton Raphson method, Iterative method, Lin Bairstow's method

Unit-II:Solution of simultaneous linear equation: Gauss elimination, Gauss Jordon, LU decomposition, Crout's Method, Jacobi, Gauss Seidel, Relaxation method, Inverse of a matrix using iterative method.

Unit-III:Finite differences: Introduction and different types of operators and relation between them, Factorial notation and polynomial in factorial notation.

Unit-IV:Interpolation: Introduction Newton forward and backward interpolation, NEwton Divided differences, Lagrange's Interpolation, Central difference intrerpolation formula, Gauss forward and backward interpolation formula. Numerical differentiation

Unit-V:Numeriacl Integration: Trapezoidal and Simpson's rules,Weddle's rule and their order of error

Unit-VI:Solution of ordinary differential eqyuation: Euler's method, Euler's modified method, Runga Kutta method, Taylor's methos. Picard's method, Adams- Bashforth method
